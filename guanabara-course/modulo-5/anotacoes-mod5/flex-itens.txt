 Property order:
All flex-items has order = 0 by default.
If you want to change the order, just put in the numeric order.

 Property align-self:
Is about the flex-items, and it is related to the cross

- align-self: auto; -- get the father's alignment (align-content or align-item)

- align-self: flex-start; -- alignment near to the cross-start

- align-self: flex-end; -- alignment near to the cross-end

- align-self: center; -- caluculate the center

- align-self: stretch; -- it will fill all the size
