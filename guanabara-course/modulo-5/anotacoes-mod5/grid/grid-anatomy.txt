A grid is a "gradiado"

grid-row != grid-line

 All is in the grid container
There are grid items. They are the small squares in the grid.

A bunch of grid items in a row or in a column is called grid track.
There is a row track or a column track

    Grid lines could be vertical or horizontal.
 The grid lines vertical are ordered by left to the right. The leftmost (the first) line is the "1".
 The horizontal grid lines are sorted by top to the bottom. The topmost is the "1".
